About Us

Our Chef

During 2020 when covid struck us. It was
one thing that united us all regardless of
our background. It was also the new trend
of small businesses to rise as many were
at home finding new ways to earn money
without risking pandemic violation and the
safety of others. Meanwhile, back at a cozy
little kitchen was a single mom dealing
with the stress of having to work online, she
wanted an outlet and so she started
experimenting and perfecting recipes.
Wanting to share her creations which at first
started as a small home business is now an
exclusive restaurant run by herself and her family.

Our Restaurant


Established in 2021, Makanan Ceylon is run
by a family that believes in freshly made food
using their own blend of spices from locally
sourced produce. We are a reservation based
restaurant so we can give you the best service.
